# Council of Ministers <orig>مجلس الوزراء</orig>
## Resolution of the Supreme Committee for the Verification of Kuwaiti Nationality <orig>اللجنة العليا لتحقيق الجنسية الكويتية</orig>
### No. (9/78 - 2) of 2025 AD
### Regarding the Loss of Kuwaiti Nationality

Having reviewed the Constitution,
And Article (10) of Amiri Decree No. 15 of 1959 regarding the Kuwaiti Nationality Law <orig>قانون الجنسية الكويتية</orig>, and its amending laws,
And after the approval of the Supreme Committee for the Verification of Kuwaiti Nationality <orig>اللجنة العليا لتحقيق الجنسية الكويتية</orig>,
We have resolved the following:

### Article One
Kuwaiti nationality shall be lost by each of the following:

<table_caption>Individuals Losing Kuwaiti Nationality</table_caption>
<table>
| No. | Name | Reference Number |
|:--|:---|:---|
| 1 | Shaima Ali Hussein Ali Al-Jana' <orig>شيماء علي حسين علي الجناع</orig> | 107894280 |
| 2 | Salwa Khalid Mahboub Al-Amer <orig>سلوى خالد محبوب العامر</orig> | 100836490 |
</table>

This is based on Article (10) of Amiri Decree No. 15 of 1959, referred to above.

### Article Two
All relevant authorities shall implement this resolution, and it shall be effective from the date of its publication in the Official Gazette <orig>الجريدة الرسمية</orig>.

# Council of Ministers <orig>مجلس الوزراء</orig>
## Decision of the Supreme Committee for Kuwaiti Nationality Verification <orig>اللجنة العليا لتحقيق الجنسية الكويتية</orig>
### No. (9/79 - 2) of 2025 AD
### Regarding the Loss of Kuwaiti Nationality

Having reviewed the Constitution,
And Article (11) of Amiri Decree No. 15 of 1959 regarding the Kuwaiti Nationality Law <orig>قانون الجنسية الكويتية</orig>, and its amending laws,
And after the approval of the Supreme Committee for Kuwaiti Nationality Verification <orig>اللجنة العليا لتحقيق الجنسية الكويتية</orig>,
We have decided the following:

### Article One
Kuwaiti nationality shall be lost by each of the following:

<table_caption>Individuals whose Kuwaiti nationality has been revoked</table_caption>
<table>
| No. | Name | Reference Number |
|:--|:---|:---|
| 1 | Dhari Sayed Mahmoud Khattar Awad <orig>ضاري سايد محمود خطار عوض</orig> | 146434163 |
| 2 | Nabwah Hussein Saad Fahad Ajimah Al-Ajmi <orig>نبوه حسين سعد فهد عجيمه العجمي</orig> | 106309019 |
| 3 | Hilal Ammash Musleh Al-Harbi <orig>هليل عماش مصلح الحربي</orig> | 101249688 |
</table>

This is based on Article (11) of Amiri Decree No. 15 of 1959, referred to above.

### Article Two
The concerned authorities shall implement this decision, each within its jurisdiction, and it shall be effective from the date of its publication in the Official Gazette.

First Deputy Prime Minister
and Minister of Interior

Decree No. (12) of 2024
Regarding the amendment of some provisions of Decree No. (15) of 1979
Concerning the Civil Service System
Having reviewed the Constitution,
And Decree-Law No. (15) of 1979 concerning the Civil Service System and its amendments,
And Decree No. (12) of 2024 amending some provisions of the aforementioned Decree No. (15) of 1979,
And based on the submission of the Prime Minister <orig>رئيس مجلس الوزراء</orig>,
And after the approval of the Council of Ministers <orig>مجلس الوزراء</orig>,
We have decreed the following:

<table>
| Article | Original Text | Amended Text |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Employees shall be appointed to vacant positions. | Employees shall be appointed to vacant positions in accordance with the provisions of this Decree. |
| 2 | The executive regulations shall specify the conditions for appointment. | The executive regulations shall specify the conditions and controls for appointment. |
</table>

Article (1)
The text of Article (1) of the aforementioned Decree No. (15) of 1979 shall be replaced with the following text:
"Employees shall be appointed to vacant positions in accordance with the provisions of this Decree."
Article (2)
The text of Article (2) of the aforementioned Decree No. (15) of 1979 shall be replaced with the following text:
"The executive regulations shall specify the conditions and controls for appointment."
Article (3)
The Prime Minister <orig>رئيس مجلس الوزراء</orig> and the Ministers – each within their respective jurisdiction – shall implement this Decree, and it shall come into force from the date of its publication in the Official Gazette <orig>الجريدة الرسمية</orig>.

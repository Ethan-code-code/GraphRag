# Council of Ministers

## Decision of the Supreme Committee for the Verification of Kuwaiti Nationality
## No. (6/63 - 2) of 2025 Regarding the Loss of Kuwaiti Nationality

Having reviewed the Constitution,
And Article (11) of Amiri Decree No. 15 of 1959 concerning the Kuwaiti Nationality Law, and its amending laws,
And upon the approval of the Supreme Committee for the Verification of Kuwaiti Nationality,
We have decided the following:

### Article One

Kuwaiti nationality is revoked from:

<table_caption>Individual whose Kuwaiti nationality is revoked</table_caption>
<table>
| No. | Name | Reference No. |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Yousef Yaqoub Saleh Abdulaziz Saleh Al-Shumaimri <orig>يوسف يعقوب صالح عبد العزيز صالح الشميمري</orig> | 122854669 |
</table>

This is based on Article (11) of the aforementioned Amiri Decree No. 15 of 1959.

### Article Two

The concerned authorities, each within its jurisdiction, shall implement this decision, and it shall come into force from the date of its publication in the Official Gazette.

First Deputy Prime Minister
and Minister of Interior
Chairman of the Supreme Committee for the Verification of Kuwaiti Nationality
Fahad Yousef Saud Al-Sabah <orig>فهد يوسف سعود الصباح</orig>

Issued on: 27 Ramadan 1446 AH
Corresponding to: March 27, 2025 AD

Decree No. 106 of 2017
Appointing an Assistant Undersecretary at the Ministry of Commerce and Industry <orig>وزارة التجارة والصناعة</orig>

- After reviewing the Constitution,
- And Law by Decree No. 15 of 1979 concerning the Civil Service and its amending laws,
- And the Decree issued on 4/4/1979 concerning the Civil Service System and its amending decrees,
- And Decree No. 116 of 1992 concerning Administrative Organization and the Determination and Delegation of Competencies,
- And upon the proposal of the Minister of Commerce and Industry <orig>وزير التجارة والصناعة</orig>,
- And after the approval of the Council of Ministers <orig>مجلس الوزراء</orig>,

We decree the following:

**Article One**

Saleh Mejbel Nama Al-Mutairi <orig>صالح مجبل نما المطيري</orig> is appointed to the grade of Assistant Undersecretary at the Ministry of Commerce and Industry <orig>وزارة التجارة والصناعة</orig>.

**Article Two**

The Prime Minister <orig>رئيس مجلس الوزراء</orig> and the Minister of Commerce and Industry <orig>وزير التجارة والصناعة</orig> shall, each within their respective jurisdictions, implement this decree. It shall come into force from the date of its issuance and shall be published in the Official Gazette <orig>الجريدة الرسمية</orig>.

**Amir of Kuwait**
**Sabah Al-Ahmad Al-Jaber Al-Sabah <orig>صباح الأحمد الجابر الصباح</orig>**

**Prime Minister**
**Jaber Al-Mubarak Al-Hamad Al-Sabah <orig>جابر المبارك الحمد الصباح</orig>**

**Minister of Commerce and Industry**
**Khaled Nasser Abdullah Al-Roudan <orig>خالد ناصر عبد الله الروضان</orig>**

Ministerial Decision No. (204) of 2017
Regarding the dissolution and liquidation of the company / Al Khosousia Holding Group (K.S.C. Holding)

The Minister of Commerce and Industry:
- After reviewing the Companies Law issued by Law No. (1) of 2016 and its executive regulations.
- And upon the minutes of the Extraordinary General Assembly meeting of the company / Al Khosousia Holding Group (K.S.C. Holding), held on 28/3/2017, which states the issuance of the Extraordinary General Assembly's resolution to dissolve and liquidate the company and to appoint Mr. Talal Abdulaziz Abdullah Al-Majed <orig>طلال عبد العزيز عبد الله الماجد</orig> as the company's liquidator.

- And based on the proposal of the Undersecretary of the Ministry.

Has decided

Article (1)
The dissolution and liquidation of the company / Al Khosousia Holding Group (K.S.C. Holding).

Article (2)
This decision shall be registered in the Commercial Register and published in the Official Gazette <orig>الجريدة الرسمية</orig>.

Article (3)
This decision shall be effective from the date of its issuance, and the competent authorities shall implement it, each within its jurisdiction.

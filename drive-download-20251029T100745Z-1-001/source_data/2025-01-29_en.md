Decree No. (12) of 2024
Regarding the amendment of some provisions of Decree No. (15) of 1979
Concerning the Civil Service System
Having reviewed the Constitution,
And Decree-Law No. (15) of 1979 concerning the Civil Service System and its amendments,
And Decree No. (12) of 2024 amending some provisions of the aforementioned Decree No. (15) of 1979,
And based on the submission of the Prime Minister <orig>رئيس مجلس الوزراء</orig>,
And after the approval of the Council of Ministers <orig>مجلس الوزراء</orig>,
We have decreed the following:

<table>
| Article | Original Text | Amended Text |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Employees shall be appointed to vacant positions. | Employees shall be appointed to vacant positions in accordance with the provisions of this Decree. |
| 2 | The executive regulations shall specify the conditions for appointment. | The executive regulations shall specify the conditions and controls for appointment. |
</table>

Article (1)
The text of Article (1) of the aforementioned Decree No. (15) of 1979 shall be replaced with the following text:
"Employees shall be appointed to vacant positions in accordance with the provisions of this Decree."
Article (2)
The text of Article (2) of the aforementioned Decree No. (15) of 1979 shall be replaced with the following text:
"The executive regulations shall specify the conditions and controls for appointment."
Article (3)
The Prime Minister <orig>رئيس مجلس الوزراء</orig> and the Ministers – each within their respective jurisdiction – shall implement this Decree, and it shall come into force from the date of its publication in the Official Gazette <orig>الجريدة الرسمية</orig>.
Issued at Seif Palace <orig>قصر السيف</orig> on: 10 Sha'ban 1445 AH <orig>10 شعبان 1445 هـ</orig>

# Council of Ministers <orig>مجلس الوزراء</orig>
## Decree No. 4 of 2025
Appointing Deputy Directors General for
the General Department of Investigations <orig>الإدارة العامة للتحقيقات</orig> at the Ministry of Interior <orig>وزارة الداخلية</orig>

- Having reviewed the Constitution <orig>الدستور</orig>,
- And the Amiri Order <orig>الأمر الأميري</orig> issued on 2 Dhu al-Qi'dah 1445 AH corresponding to May 10, 2024 AD,
- And Decree-Law <orig>المرسوم بالقانون</orig> No. 15 of 1979 concerning Civil Service <orig>الخدمة المدنية</orig> and its amending laws,
- And the Law Regulating the Judiciary <orig>قانون تنظيم القضاء</orig> issued by Decree-Law <orig>المرسوم بقانون</orig> No. 23 of 1990, and its amending laws,
- And Law No. 53 of 2001 concerning the General Department of Investigations <orig>الإدارة العامة للتحقيقات</orig> at the Ministry of Interior <orig>وزارة الداخلية</orig>,
- And the Decree issued on 7 Jumada al-Ula <orig>جمادى الأولى</orig> 1399 AH corresponding to April 4, 1979 AD concerning the Civil Service System <orig>نظام الخدمة المدنية</orig> and its amending decrees,
- And Decree No. 84 of 2002 regarding the salaries, allowances, and bonuses <orig>مرتبات وبدلات وعلاوات</orig> of members of the General Department of Investigations <orig>الإدارة العامة للتحقيقات</orig>, and its amending decrees,
- And based on the submission of the First Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Interior <orig>النائب الأول لرئيس مجلس الوزراء ووزير الداخلية</orig>,
- And after the approval of the Council of Ministers <orig>مجلس الوزراء</orig>,

We have decreed the following:
### Article One
The following shall be appointed as Deputy Directors General for the General Department of Investigations <orig>الإدارة العامة للتحقيقات</orig> at the Ministry of Interior <orig>وزارة الداخلية</orig>:
1.  Public Prosecutor <orig>مدعي عام</orig> / Saad Mohammed Ayyad Al-Mutairi <orig>سعد محمد عياد المطيري</orig>.
2.  Public Prosecutor <orig>مدعي عام</orig> / Reem Barrak Abdul Mohsen Al-Mousa <orig>ريم براك عبد المحسن الموسى</orig>.
3.  Public Prosecutor <orig>مدعي عام</orig> / Bassam Mohammed Al-Mishaan Al-Mashouh <orig>بسام محمد المشعان المشوح</orig>.

### Article Two
The First Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Interior <orig>النائب الأول لرئيس مجلس الوزراء ووزير الداخلية</orig> shall implement this Decree, which shall be effective from the date of its issuance and published in the Official Gazette <orig>الجريدة الرسمية</orig>.

Amir of Kuwait <orig>أمير الكويت</orig>
Mishal Al-Ahmad Al-Jaber Al-Sabah <orig>مشعل الأحمد الجابر الصباح</orig>

Prime Minister <orig>رئيس مجلس الوزراء</orig>

First Deputy Prime Minister
and Minister of Interior <orig>وزير الداخلية</orig>

Decree No. (12) of 2024
Regarding the amendment of some provisions of Decree No. (15) of 1979
Concerning the Civil Service System
Having reviewed the Constitution,
And Decree-Law No. (15) of 1979 concerning the Civil Service System and its amendments,
And Decree No. (12) of 2024 amending some provisions of the aforementioned Decree No. (15) of 1979,
And based on the submission of the Prime Minister <orig>رئيس مجلس الوزراء</orig>,
And after the approval of the Council of Ministers <orig>مجلس الوزراء</orig>,
We have decreed the following:

<table>
| Article | Original Text | Amended Text |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Employees shall be appointed to vacant positions. | Employees shall be appointed to vacant positions in accordance with the provisions of this Decree. |
| 2 | The executive regulations shall specify the conditions for appointment. | The executive regulations shall specify the conditions and controls for appointment. |
</table>

Article (1)
The text of Article (1) of the aforementioned Decree No. (15) of 1979 shall be replaced with the following text:
"Employees shall be appointed to vacant positions in accordance with the provisions of this Decree."
Article (2)
The text of Article (2) of the aforementioned Decree No. (15) of 1979 shall be replaced with the following text:
"The executive regulations shall specify the conditions and controls for appointment."
Article (3)
The Prime Minister <orig>رئيس مجلس الوزراء</orig> and the Ministers – each within their respective jurisdiction – shall implement this Decree, and it shall come into force from the date of its publication in the Official Gazette <orig>الجريدة الرسمية</orig>.

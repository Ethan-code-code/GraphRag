### Decree No. (68) of 2024
### on the Formation of the Cabinet

Having reviewed the Constitution,
And the Amiri Order issued on 25 Ramadan 1445 AH, corresponding to 4 April 2024 AD, appointing the Prime Minister,
And upon the proposal of the Prime Minister,

**We have decreed the following:**

**Article One**

The following are appointed:

1.  Fahad Yousef Saud Al-Sabah <orig>فهد يوسف سعود الصباح</orig> - as First Deputy Prime Minister, Minister of Defense, and Minister of Interior.
2.  Shereeda Abdullah Saad Al-Mousharji <orig>شريدة عبد الله سعد المعوشرجي</orig> - as Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of State for Cabinet Affairs.
3.  Dr. Imad Mohammad Abdulaziz Al-Ateeqi <orig>عماد محمد عبد العزيز العتيقي</orig> - as Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Oil.
4.  Abdulrahman Badah Abdulrahman Al-Mutairi <orig>عبد الرحمن بداح عبد الرحمن المطيري</orig> - as Minister of Information and Culture.
5.  Dr. Ahmed Abdulwahab Ahmed Al-Awadhi <orig>أحمد عبد الوهاب أحمد العوضي</orig> - as Minister of Health.
6.  Dr. Anwar Ali Abdullah Al-Mudhaf <orig>أنور علي عبد الله المضف</orig> - as Minister of Finance and Minister of State for Economic Affairs and Investment.
7.  Dr. Adel Mohammad Abdullah Al-Adwani <orig>عادل محمد عبد الله العدواني</orig> - as Minister of Education and Minister of Higher Education and Scientific Research.
8.  Abdullah Ali Abdullah Al-Yahya <orig>عبد الله علي عبد الله اليحيا</orig> - as Minister of Foreign Affairs.
9.  Dr. Noura Mohammad Khaled Al-Mashaan <orig>نورة محمد خالد المشعان</orig> - as Minister of Public Works and Minister of State for Municipal Affairs.
10. Dr. Mohammad Ibrahim Mohammad Al-Wasmi <orig>محمد إبراهيم محمد الوسمي</orig> - as Minister of Justice and Minister of Awqaf and Islamic Affairs.
11. Omar Saud Abdulaziz Al-Omar <orig>عمر سعود عبد العزيز العمر</orig> - as Minister of Commerce and Industry and Minister of State for Communications Affairs.
12. Dr. Mahmoud Abdulaziz Mahmoud Bushehri <orig>محمود عبد العزيز محمود بوشهري</orig> - as Minister of Electricity, Water, and Renewable Energy and Minister of State for Housing Affairs.
13. Dr. Amthal Hadi Hif Al-Huwailah <orig>أمثال هادي هايف الحويلة</orig> - as Minister of Social Affairs, Labor, Family and Childhood Affairs, and Minister of State for Youth Affairs.

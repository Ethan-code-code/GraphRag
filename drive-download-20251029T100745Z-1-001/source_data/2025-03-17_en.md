### Ministry of Commerce and Industry <orig>وزارة التجارة والصناعة</orig>

**Decree No. 314 of 2017**
**Concerning the Approval of the Agreement between the Government of the State of Kuwait and the Government of the Republic of Iraq on the Promotion and Reciprocal Protection of Investments**

- After reviewing the Constitution,
- And Law No. 4 of 1962 concerning Patents, Industrial Designs, and Models,
- And Law No. 20 of 2014 concerning Electronic Transactions,
- And Law No. 37 of 2014 establishing the Communication and Information Technology Regulatory Authority,
- And Law No. 116 of 2013 concerning the Promotion of Direct Investment in the State of Kuwait,
- And Amiri Decree <orig>المرسوم الأميري</orig> No. 12 of 1960 enacting the Law on the Organization of the Fatwa and Legislation Department of the Government of Kuwait,
- And the Agreement between the Government of the State of Kuwait and the Government of the Republic of Iraq on the Promotion and Reciprocal Protection of Investments,
- And based on the proposal of the Minister of Commerce and Industry <orig>وزير التجارة والصناعة</orig>,
- And after the approval of the Council of Ministers <orig>مجلس الوزراء</orig>,

**We have decreed the following:**

**Article One**

The Agreement between the Government of the State of Kuwait and the Government of the Republic of Iraq on the Promotion and Reciprocal Protection of Investments, and the Protocol annexed thereto, both signed in Kuwait City on May 20, 2017, the texts of which are attached to this Decree, are hereby approved.

**Article Two**

The Prime Minister <orig>رئيس مجلس الوزراء</orig> and the Ministers—each within their respective jurisdictions—shall implement this Decree, and it shall come into force from the date of its publication in the Official Gazette.

**Amir of Kuwait**
**Sabah Al-Ahmad Al-Jaber Al-Sabah <orig>صباح الأحمد الجابر الصباح</orig>**

**Prime Minister <orig>رئيس مجلس الوزراء</orig>**
**Jaber Al-Mubarak Al-Hamad Al-Sabah <orig>جابر المبارك الحمد الصباح</orig>**

**Minister of Commerce and Industry <orig>وزير التجارة والصناعة</orig>**
**Khaled Nasser Abdullah Al-Roudan <orig>خالد ناصر عبد الله الروضان</orig>**

Issued at Seif Palace on: 20 Rabi' al-Awwal 1439 H
Corresponding to: 8 December 2017 AD

<h2>Decree No. 4 of 2024</h2>
<h2>Appointing the Chairman of the Board of Directors and Director General of the Environment Public Authority</h2>

Having reviewed the Constitution,
And Law No. 42 of 2014 concerning Environmental Protection, as amended by Law No. 99 of 2015,
And Decree No. 275 of 2015 on the formation of the Board of Directors of the Environment Public Authority,
And Decree No. 19 of 2020 appointing the Chairman of the Board of Directors and Director General of the Environment Public Authority,
And upon the proposal of the Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Oil,
And after the approval of the Council of Ministers,

<h2>We have decreed the following:</h2>

**Article One**

Sheikh Abdullah Ahmad Al-Humoud Al-Sabah <orig>الشيخ/ عبد الله أحمد الحمود الصباح</orig> is hereby appointed as Chairman of the Board of Directors and Director General of the Environment Public Authority <orig>الهيئة العامة للبيئة</orig>, with the rank of Undersecretary, for a term of four years.
